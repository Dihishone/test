# Trufflepig IT-Forensics — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark corporate cyber-forensics. Near-black navy canvas with restrained warm-amber accents (the "truffle" signal) and cool steel-teal for technical/data tone. Feels premium, institutional, serious — executive briefing, not social ad. Typography is confident display sans paired with forensic mono. Motion is slow, authoritative, deliberate: `power3.out`, `expo.out`, `sine.inOut`. Nothing playful, nothing neon-cyber-cliché.

## Colors

- `#0a0e15` — background (near-black, cool navy tint)
- `#e8eaed` — foreground (off-white)
- `#d79a4a` — accent primary (truffle amber — brand-evocative, premium)
- `#4a8fb8` — accent secondary (steel teal — tech/data)
- `#7a8291` — muted (steel gray for labels, meta)
- `#1a2130` — card/panel surface

## Typography

- **Archivo 900** — display headlines (condensed-feel grotesk, authoritative)
- **IBM Plex Mono 300 / 400** — body, labels, data, metadata (forensic precision)

Pairing tension: institutional authority (Archivo 900) × clinical forensic precision (Plex Mono 300). Sans + mono crosses class — no double-sans.

## Motion

- Transitions: blur crossfade, 0.5s, `power2.inOut` (premium/tech default)
- Entrances: `power3.out`, `expo.out`, `power4.out`, `back.out`, `sine.out` — vary per scene
- Persistent ambient decoratives: scan grid, radial amber glow, brand stamp

## What NOT to Do

- No neon cyan/magenta gradients
- No Matrix-style falling characters
- No playful emoji or glyphs
- No Inter/Roboto/Poppins — banned
- No left-edge accent stripes or gradient text
- No pure `#000` or `#fff` — always tinted
