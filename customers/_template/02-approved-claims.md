## Forbidden Claim Patterns (Global)

These patterns are forbidden in all videos regardless of claim ID:

- "The only [X]" — unless competitor analysis is current and cited
- "Best-in-class" — unless Gartner / Forrester / analyst citation attached
- "Market-leading" — unless analyst ranking cited
- "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" — no exceptions
- Any ROI or cost saving claim without a named customer source or internal study
- Any security certification claim not current as of shoot date
